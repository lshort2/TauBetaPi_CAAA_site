Mission

RSI’s mission, since incorporating in 1996, is to assist government agencies realize their business objectives and achieve significant results from information technology investments. RSI is a software provider of mission critical integrated tax and compliance software for international, state/province, county/city government and partners with clients on IT initiatives to improve customer service, increase operational efficiency and maximize compliance.
